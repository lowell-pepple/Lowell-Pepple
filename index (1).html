About

Who’s writing this

I’m Lowell Pepple, a finance student transferring into the USC Marshall School of Business. I’m focused on a path into investment banking and, longer term, private equity — and this site is where I do the reps that classes don’t require.

Before Marshall I worked at a law firm and spent time around M&A advisory, which is where I first got interested in how deals actually come together. What holds my attention now is a narrower question: what makes a business genuinely durable — the kind you’d want to own for a decade, not rent for a quarter.

Every note here is my own work, built from public information. I’m early in this, the analysis will sharpen over time, and I’d welcome a sharp pushback on anything I’ve gotten wrong.
